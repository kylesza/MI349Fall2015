Blog Style Site for Podcast

Elevator Pitch

My friends from home and I just started up a podcast, and I currently have a very basic set-up. I’d like to have more of a blog style website that would have separate posts for each episode, maybe with a type of comment section. There would be a type of backlog area where it’d be easier for the user to see older episodes. Also there would be an “About Us” section that might have separate pages for each of the hosts. Overall, an easy to navigate website that keeps users on our site, and not to redirect them somewhere else to listen.
